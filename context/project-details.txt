Use Case Overview

SpaceShare — Subscription-Based Co-working Space Marketplace

THE PROBLEM

Remote workers & freelancers need flexible, affordable workspace beyond home.

Traditional leases: too costly. Coffee shops: unreliable.

THE SOLUTION

SpaceShare — a two-sided marketplace where hosts pay a subscription to list spaces; guests browse & book by hour or day with no hidden fees.

Small business owners, building managers with spare space. Pay a subscription to list and monetise their property.

Freelancers, remote workers, small teams. Search, filter, and book available spaces by hour or day.

Platform manager. Approves listings, manages subscriptions, monitors analytics and platform health.

FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS


Host Subscription & Listing

Space Discovery & Search

Booking & Slot Management

Reviews & Ratings

Admin Dashboard
